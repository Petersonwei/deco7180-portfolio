Project - DingDingGo

Our project is a website that allows our users, university students, to be able to search up events in Brisbane and connect with their friends to attend events together. The features we have designed consider the characteristics of our Target Audience. Our research has shown that they value socializing, customized experiences, and exploring. Our project will be considered a success if our users find the website intuitive and enjoy using the website. They should also be able to connect with their peers thereby fulfilling the social aspect. It will also be a success if the features encourage repeated and prolonged usage of the website to explore new events. My teammates will discuss how our features satisfy the above criteria, and we will validate this through further user testing in the coming weeks. Currently our mvp prioritizes structure and functionality, which is why the design of it is still quite barebone.
